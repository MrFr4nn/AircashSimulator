Aircash business site (back-office portal)


Aircash back-office portal is a web-based application used for tracking transactions that are processed on the Aircash system.

To open an account, a partner needs to send a request to info@aircash.eu containing an email of the user. Aircash will create an account and send credentials (username and password) to the email from the request. After the first login, a user will be requested to change his password.

After a successful login, the user will be able to use the following features:

  1. Transactions – table with all successful transactions on the Aircash system. Transactions can be filtered (transaction id, location id, period or service – issued and canceled) and exported to CSV
  2. Coupon – used for coupon cancellation. Each coupon can be canceled by entering the serial number of the coupon.

Technical requirements for using Aircash back office portal are access to the Internet and a web browser (Chrome recommended).
