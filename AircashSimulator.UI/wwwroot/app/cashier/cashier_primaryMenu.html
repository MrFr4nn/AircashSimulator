Aircash Frame

Deposit to your online account by using the Abon voucher or by using the Aircash app. Withdraw your online funds to Aircash digital wallet.

Abon

Make a deposit to your online account or to Aircash digital wallet via Abon voucher by entering a 16-digit coupon code or scanning the QR code on it.

C2D flow

Make a deposit of your cash money to your online account.

Aircash Payout

Withdraw your online funds to Aircash digital wallet by entering your phone number and the amount you wish to withdraw.

Aircash Pay

Make online purchases or top up your online account via Aircash Pay.

Aircash Payment

Make a deposit to your online account directly from Aircash's Marketplace by entering the username or email address as an identifier as well as the amount you wish to deposit.

Aircash C2D Payout

Withdraw your online funds at point of sales.

Sales Partner

Make a deposit of your cash money to your Aircash digital wallet or withdraw cash money on the cash register at the point of sales.
