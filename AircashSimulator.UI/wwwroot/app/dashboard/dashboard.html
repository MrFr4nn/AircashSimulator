General

Aircash is a financial platform for the transfer of money, and at the same time a licensed payment institution with an approval issued by the Croatian National Bank, valid in the European Union. In addition to the technical aspects, the platform meets the required rolling stock regulations and procedures for preventing money laundering.
Functionally, the Aircash platform enables fast and easy transfer of money between different users, regardless of whether they are a legal or a natural person.

The purpose of this document is to provide documentation for the integration of the payment process to the partner system through the Aircash application and for the integration of the payment process where the user pays the money from the partner system to the Aircash system.

Technical connectivity features

Access

The Aircash system allows the HTTPS / TLS 1.2 protocol on its access points. Aircash system is restricting its access points by the IP address of the partner. Partner is obliged to submit static (one or more) IPs that will access the Aircash test environment.

Authentication

Authentication is achieved with digital signature on each request. In case of payout, the partner will need to generate an X.509 certificate and use a private key to sign partners requests towards Aircash. Partner will need to send the public key to Aircash, and Aircash will use that public key to verify the digital signature on each request. We discuss in detail how to generate certificates and how to generate digital signatures in latter chapters. In case of transaction confirmation, Aircash uses Aircash private key to sign requests towards partners. Aircash will give the partner its public key, and the partner will need to use that public key to verify the digital signature on each request. In case of code generation and cancellation Partner will use his private key to sign the request and Aircash will use the public key to verify the digital signature.
