General


Aircash is a financial platform for the transfer of money, and at the same time a licensed payment institution with an approval issued by the Croatian National Bank, valid in the European Union. In addition to the technical aspects, the platform meets the required rolling stock regulations and procedures for preventing money laundering.
Functionally, the Aircash platform enables fast and easy transfer of money between different users, regardless of whether they are a legal or a natural person.

Main Aircash products


Abon voucher

  • QR code/16-digit paper voucher issued on more than 100.000 POS in the EU
  • Used for Aircash cash app deposits and payments to the online accounts

Aircash cash app

  • Downloadable free mobile application
  • Used for money transfer, payments and deposits & withdrawals to online accounts
  • Enables cash deposits at Aircash POS and cash withdrawals with Aircash Mastercard

Aircash Mastercard

  • Prepaid Mastercard payment card currently sold on more than 35.000 POS in the EU
  • Enables payments and ATM withdrawals
  • Connected, personalized (KYC) and controlled, by using the Aircash app

Aircash user streams


Money transfers

  • Money transfers enables users to transfer money to their relatives and friends.
  • It's used by expats and immigrants in Western Europe to transfer money to their families in Eastern Europe and Turkey.

Day-to-day services

  • Have one app that enables the best customer experience with one-click deposit and one-click withdrawal to/from an online account
  • Purchase PlayStation and Xbox vouchers, purchase bus and ferry tickets, pay parking, purchase vignettes and tolls, buy gifts cards and telecom vouchers, pay utility bills…
  • With Aircash Marketplace promotions users have added benefits like discounts, cashback, bonuses, etc.

Food delivery couriers

  • Food delivery companies, like Wolt and Glovo, accept cash payments and all their food delivery couriers need to deposit cash into the Company account.
  • Instead of waiting in line in a bank, couriers use Aircash to deposit money directly into the Company account.
