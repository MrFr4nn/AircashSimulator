General


Aircash is a financial platform for the transfer of money, and at the same time a licensed payment institution with an approval issued by the Croatian National Bank, valid in the European Union. In addition to the technical aspects, the platform meets the required rolling stock regulations and procedures for preventing money laundering.
Functionally, the Aircash platform enables fast and easy transfer of money between different users, regardless of whether they are a legal or a natural person.

Aircash Integration Portal

Aircash Integration Portal is a help center where partner go to get information and raise requests.

Technical integration support for Aircash integrations is organized through Aircash Integration Portal.

Getting access

In order to get get help from Aircash integration team you will need an access to the Aircash integration portal which can be obtained in the following way:

  1. Send an email to tech@aircash.eu and list of participants needed under CC
  2. Get na invite from the Aircash integration team on your email if the list is already provided to the Aircash team

You will get the invite from the jira@aircash.atlassian.net asking you to to finish your account (check if this address needs to be whitelisted)

Welcome!

You've been invited to Aircash integration portal. You can use this to raise requests and get help.

To finish setting up your account, simply click on your sign-up link.

Opening ticket

With the active account, you can raise a technical question by selecting Integration request:

Integration request

Technical question or request regarding Aircash integration


In the form enter Summary, Description and Attachment (if needed) and submit the button. Soon after submission, you will get confrimation email from jira@aircash.atlassian.net containing the shortcut links to the request on the Aircash Integration Portal.

Exceptionally, if there would be a need to open a ticket from the Aircash side (when partner needs to make an action or provide an answer) you will get the email saying that the Aircash team has opened a ticket and that there is a action required

Processing ticket

Once opened, the ticket will be queued and when the Aircash integration team makes a reply, you will be notified via email (if notificiations are turned on)

If the request needs more information or there are multiple steps to complete it, we will keep the ticket opened and discuss everything needed via request thread. Please bear in mind not to mix integration topics on the single request thread. Feel free to open a new request for each topic that needs to be discussed

Examples of the requests

  • Need to whitelist these IPs
  • Getting 401 response on the CheckUser request
  • Need Aircash test application
  • Check the status of the transaction

Closing the ticket

Once the requested action is completed by the Aircash integration team, or the topic is discussed on the request thread, Aircash team will close the ticket and you will be notified via email notification

On the Integration Portal you can find your all tickets, including the ones previously closed.
