Abon amount options

Partner can choose one of the following options concerning the Abon amount parametar:

  1. Partner sends 0 or NULL as Abon amount. In that case, Aircash system will not validate the amount and the transaction will be accepted.
  2. Partner sends the exact amount. The transaction will be successful if the amount matches the value of the Abon.
  3. Partner sends the maximum amount they will accept. The transaction will be successful if the value of the Abon is equal to or less than the maximum.
