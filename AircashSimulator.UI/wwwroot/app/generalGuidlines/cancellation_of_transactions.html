Cancellation of transactions


Regarding the cancellation, it is done always manually, in coordination with Aircash Customer Support.

If the transaction is successful, and later on needs to be canceled, we need to receive (via email sent to info@aircash.eu) the transaction details (transaction ids, date and time, amount) of the transaction that needs to be canceled.

The flow/procedure for the Aircash Payment (Aircash marketplace deposit) and Aircash Pay via Aircash Frame would be:

  • The user makes a deposit of 100 EUR and the funds are approved on his (merchant’s) account
  • The user requires a cancellation/refund
    • if the user asks Aircash customer support to make a refund, we will redirect him to merchant customer support
  • Merchant customer support marks the transaction as canceled (funds deducted from his online account
  • Merchant customer support sends the email to Aircash customer support asking for the cancellation
  • Aircash customer support cancels the transaction and the funds are back in the Aircash wallet

The flow/procedure for the Aircash Payout (withdrawal) would be:

  • The user makes a withdrawal of 100 EUR and the funds are approved on his Aircash wallet
  • The user requires a cancellation/refund
  • Merchant customer support sends the email to Aircash customer support asking for the cancellation
    • note that in the withdrawal this is an important step - funds first need to be deducted from his Aircash wallet
    • Aircash customer support will be able to cancel the withdrawal only if the funds are still available on the Aircash wallet
  • After confirmation from Aircash Customer support, Merchant customer support can mark the transaction as canceled (funds are returned to the user’s online account)

Abon deposits cannot be canceled or refunded, after the voucher is consumed its state cannot be changed.
