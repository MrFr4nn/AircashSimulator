Reconciliation process


We have 4 different methods on how you can get reconciliation files:

  1. You can get them sent to your email
  2. We can push it to our FTP
  3. We can push it to your FTP/SFTP
  4. You can find them in our BS portal (Business Site Portal) through GUI

Explanation of table fields:

  • “CreatedOnDateTimeUTC“ - date and time when is a transaction created (UTC)
  • “AircashTransactionID“ - Unique Id of the transaction in the Aircash system
  • “Service“ - AircashPayout, AircashPayment, AbonUsed, AbonIssued, AbonCancelled, AircashPaymentCancellation, AircashPayoutCancellation
  • “Amount“ - transaction amount
  • “Fee” - transaction fee
  • “PartnerTransactionID“ - Unique Id of the transaction in the Partner system
  • “LocationID“ - Identifier of the point of sale
