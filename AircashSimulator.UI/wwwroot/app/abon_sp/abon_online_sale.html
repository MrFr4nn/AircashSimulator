Abon online sale

  • Point of sale (online shop) must check that the end customer is an individual (not a legal entity) and that purchase is not being conducted for commercial purposes, especially but not limited to the purpose of further re-selling of Abon.
  • Point of Sale (online shop) shall provide and assign to each end customer the unique identifier, “UserID”.
  • UserIId data should be provided at request to Aircash in case of e.g. fraud or money laundering or terrorist financing suspicion.
  • In the case of the online shop, the UserId parameter needs to be sent under the PointOfSaleId parameter in the request.
  • UserId can be any unique identifier of the user in the online shop (id, UUID).
